I am final year Masters student in Statistics at [Department of Mathematics & Statistics](https://iitk.ac.in/math/), Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. My primary research interests include Methodological Statistics, Robust Statistical Analysis, Bayesian Analysis and Time Series. I love working in domains which help me explore new concepts and techniques in Statistics and Mathematics.

I hail from Jalpaiguri, a town in West Bengal, where I spent the entire duration of my school life. I spent 3 years in Kolkata for my Bachelors degree and currently, after the ever long pandemic, I am inside the campus of IIT Kanpur! I am fortunate enough to be advised by [Prof. Amit Mitra](https://home.iitk.ac.in/~amitra/), who guided me through my first research project, introducing me to problems pertaining to robust and parsimonious signal processing models. I will be joining [University of Pittsburgh](https://www.pitt.edu/) as a PhD student starting this Fall ('22).

Apart from the academic pursuits, I am very passionate about music and cricket. I love playing piano in my spare time.
